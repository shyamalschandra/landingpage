Official landing page of Shyamal Chandra

Born in Pittsburg (Kansas) in 1983, Shyamal Suhana Chandra started learning piano at the tender age of four until twelve, earning only gold medals at regional piano competitions. Currently, he loves composing lyrics, generating melodies, and producing hits on Logic Pro X, Garageband (MacOS, iPadOS), and Bandlab. He publishes tunes on Bandcamp and all major music outlets around the world. He loves sports, board games, nonfiction books, news, and R&D in addition to a full-time career in software engineering. He attended University of Pittsburgh as a University Scholar while getting a B.S. in Computer Science (Summa Cum Laude) with departmental honors from 2001 to 2005, Carnegie Mellon University as a Visiting Scholar in 2004, and UCLA PhD program in Computer Science as a Graduate Fellow between 2005 to 2006. Shyamal loves running, dancing, singing, composing, choreography, and directing in addition to group exercise (kickboxing, U-Jam, Zumba, and Bollywood bhangra).
